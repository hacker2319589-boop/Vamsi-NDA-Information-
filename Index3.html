NDA (National Defence Academy) exam is conducted by UPSC for joining Indian Army, Navy and Air Force.

This website provides complete preparation guidance including syllabus, physical standards, SSB interview tips and study plans.

Eligibility & Qualification

  • Age: 16.5 to 19.5 years
  • Education: 12th Pass
  • For Air Force & Navy: Physics and Mathematics compulsory
  • Only unmarried male & female candidates eligible

NDA Syllabus & Chapter Weightage

Mathematics (300 Marks)

  • Algebra – 20%
  • Trigonometry – 15%
  • Calculus – 20%
  • Vectors & 3D – 10%
  • Probability & Statistics – 10%

General Ability Test (600 Marks)

  • English – 200 Marks
  • Physics – 100 Marks
  • Chemistry – 60 Marks
  • History – 80 Marks
  • Geography – 80 Marks
  • Current Affairs – 80 Marks

Physical Test Details

  • Good stamina and endurance
  • Running practice recommended
  • Pushups, situps, pullups
  • Outdoor group tasks during SSB

Medical Test Details

  • Good eyesight
  • No major bone deformities
  • Normal hearing ability
  • Proper height & weight ratio

SSB Interview Tricks & Communication

  • Be confident and honest
  • Improve English communication
  • Practice PPDT and TAT stories
  • Develop leadership qualities
  • Stay calm in group discussion

Board members check Officer Like Qualities (OLQs) such as responsibility, teamwork and decision making.

Study Timetable

  • Morning: Mathematics (2 hrs)
  • Afternoon: GAT Subjects (2 hrs)
  • Evening: Revision (1 hr)
  • Night: Current Affairs (30 mins)

Previous Year Questions (Practice Topics)

  • Solve last 10 years Mathematics papers
  • Practice English grammar questions
  • Revise Physics numericals

Fitness Tips

  • Daily running 1-2 km
  • Pushups 20 × 3 sets
  • Healthy diet with proteins
  • Drink plenty of water

Motivation

"Discipline is the bridge between goals and success."
